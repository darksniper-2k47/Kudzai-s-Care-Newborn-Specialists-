Meet Kudzai — Your Family's Newborn Care Partner

Our Story

Kudzai founded Kudzai's Newborn Care Agency after years of witnessing new parents struggle through the most beautiful yet overwhelming chapter of their lives — alone and exhausted.

As a Certified Newborn Care Specialist with advanced postpartum doula training, lactation counselor certification, and infant sleep specialist qualifications, Kudzai brings deep expertise and genuine warmth to every family she serves.

Her philosophy is simple: when parents are rested and supported, the whole family thrives.

Every specialist in the agency is personally selected and trained to Kudzai's exacting standards — because your family deserves nothing less.
